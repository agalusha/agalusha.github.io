Welcome

Steve Hensley is an actor/comedian, based in Chicago, IL. He has been performing since around the age of six, and has performed in various settings across the United States.  In addition to performing his routines, Steve has taught comedy improv workshops to hundreds of youth of varying skill levels.

 

Steve brings a fresh approach to his comedy through unexpected twists that leave the audience wondering what he will come up with next.

 

Steve is a graduate of Graceland University, where he studied Theatre, Film, and Education.

"I like to make people laugh who are normally serious"                        -Jimmy Fallon
